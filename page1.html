A bit About Me

rather individualistic perfectionist, who chooses to work by themselves, mostly enjoying grunge/punkish-rock aesthetics & dark academia; that's why i plan to go to a greater extent into this way in the future, when i will already collect my whole gear, and not just the phone, as my main photo-taking device. in the current situation, I move in this aesthetic by making dark, dim, monochromatic creations, like photos of old buildings & ruins, or darker sceneries in general. hopefully soon, I will be able to publish something bigger and overall better.

at the moment, I focus more on writing and digital art, so I often put taking photos aside, and just doing it while my daily life ruts outside, when something specific catches my eye. writing, creating scenarios and stories with my own characters, music and going to concerts of my liked bands, overall nature, like herbalism and mushrooms, crystals; i can call all of them my little interests.
